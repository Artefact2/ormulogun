What is Ormulogun?

Ormulogun is a tactics trainer for chess. It is completely free and libre.

Puzzles are generated from tactics in classical games of high-rated players on Lichess.

Ormulogun is still under heavy developement. Some features are incomplete, or not implemented at all. Because puzzles are computer-generated and computer-tagged, some may not make sense to humans.
