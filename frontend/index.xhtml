What is Ormulogun?

Ormulogun is a tactics trainer for chess. It is completely free and libre. Progress is kept in your browser ; it can be saved and restored later in other browsers or computers.

Puzzles are arranged using the Leitner system, a simple way of doing spaced repetition. You will be prompted puzzles that you have failed in the past at regular intervals until you consistently get correct answers.

Puzzles are generated from classical games of high-rated players on Lichess.
